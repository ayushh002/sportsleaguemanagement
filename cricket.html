                                Legend Players
MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a former Indian international cricketer who is considered one of the greatest captains in the history of Indian cricket. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand), India, Dhoni is known for his calm and composed demeanor on the field and his astute captaincy.Dhoni is a Legend because under his captaincy, India achieved significant success, winning major tournaments, including the ICC World Twenty20 in 2007, the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013. His leadership style, tactical acumen, and ability to remain calm under pressure earned him widespread praise.

Viv Richards

Sir Vivian Richards, commonly known as Viv Richards, is a former West Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport. He was born on March 7, 1952, in St. John's, Antigua.Viv Richards was known for his powerful and stylish batting. He played with a fearless approach and was particularly aggressive against fast bowlers. His ability to dominate the best bowling attacks of his time set him apart as one of the greats of the game.He played a crucial role in the West Indies' World Cup victories in 1975 and 1979.

Donald Bradman

Sir Donald Bradman, often referred to as "The Don," is widely considered one of the greatest cricketers in the history of the sport. He was born on August 27, 1908, in Cootamundra, New South Wales, Australia, and passed away on February 25, 2001. Bradman's most remarkable achievement is his batting average, which stands at 99.94 in Test cricket. This is an extraordinary figure and is significantly higher than that of any other player. His consistency and ability to score heavily in almost every innings set him apart. In the 1930 Ashes series in England, Bradman set a record for the highest individual score in Test cricket at that time by scoring 334 at Headingley, Leeds.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India. Tendulkar's international cricket career spanned from 1989 to 2013.Tendulkar holds numerous records in international cricket, including being the all-time leading run-scorer in both Test and One Day International (ODI) formats. He is the only player to have scored one hundred international centuries (100 centuries combined in Tests and ODIs).Tendulkar played 200 Test matches, scoring over 15,000 runs, and he remains the leading run-scorer in Test cricket for India.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is considered one of the best batsmen in the world. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has achieved significant success in international cricket.Kohli is known for his exceptional batting skills and has set numerous records. He is one of the fastest batsmen to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs).Kohli has received several awards for his performances, including the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, India's highest sporting honor, and the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times.
