Badminton


Introduction

Badminton is a racquet sport played either in singles (one player per side) or doubles (two players per side), where players or pairs take turns hitting a shuttlecock over a net. The game can be played both recreationally and competitively, with organized leagues and tournaments held worldwide.Badminton is a fast-paced and physically demanding sport that requires agility, speed, and strategic thinking.Badminton is played at various levels, from casual games at local clubs to international competitions like the Olympic Games, where it has been a part of the program since 1992. It is a popular recreational activity and a highly competitive sport enjoyed by millions of people worldwide.


                                Legend Players
Lin Dan

Lin Dan is the most successful badminton player in the history of this sport. He has literally won everything he could with his 35 years of age. Starting with a victory at Korea Open in 2002, Lin Dan has become two-time Olympic champion.He is one of the best defensive players ever seen known for fabulous movement and unrealistic returns. Other notable achievements are a five-time champion of the world and impressive six-time All-England champion! He is also the first man who defended his Olympic Gold in 2012.

Peter Gade

Peter Hoeg Gade is a retired Denmark player who was dominating from 1998 to 2006. He has an impressive score for a European competitor - 22 Grand-Prix titles.Peter was a five times European champion, four times bronze and one-time silver medalist at the World Championships. He also won at Korea Open champion four times, and All-England Championship once.His trademarks were very fast attacks and deceptive shots which were confusing even the best players in the world. Some people even call him a “trick shot expert”.

Viktor Axelsen

Viktor Axelsen is a 29-year-old badminton player from Denmark who shocks the world over and over with his achievements. Many players are not even close to his ranking, and he storms Europe.Viktor even parries the most legendary players in the world. He claims his most memorable title is the one he won at 2010's Men Singles Champion. He is known for outstanding trick shots.His achievement are - 2016 Olympic Games bronze medal winner, BWF World Championships gold medal in 2017, bronze medal in 2014, European Championships (first place in 2016 and 2018, third place in 2012, 2014, 2017).
