Tennis


Introduction

Tennis is a racket sport that is played either between two individuals (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). The game has a simple objective: to score points by successfully hitting the ball in such a way that the opponent is unable to return it within the boundaries of the court. Tennis is played on a rectangular court with a net in the middle.Tennis can be played on different surfaces, including grass, clay, and hard court. The dimensions of the court are standardized.The court is divided into two halves, with a net in the middle. Each half is further divided into service courts.


                                Legend Players
Sania Mirza

Sania Mirza is a former professional tennis player from India who is considered one of the country's greatest tennis stars. Born on November 15, 1986, in Mumbai, India, she gained international recognition for her accomplishments in women's doubles and mixed doubles.Mirza achieved significant success in women's doubles and mixed doubles. She won a number of Grand Slam titles in doubles events.Over the course of her career, Mirza achieved the World No. 1 ranking in women's doubles, becoming the first Indian female player to do so.

Roger Federer

Roger Federer is a professional tennis player from Switzerland who is widely regarded as one of the greatest tennis players of all time. Born on August 8, 1981, in Basel, Switzerland, Federer has had an illustrious career and has achieved numerous records and milestones.Federer has won a total of 20 Grand Slam singles titles, which is tied for the most by any male player in history. He has won each of the four Grand Slam tournaments: the Australian Open, the French Open, Wimbledon, and the US Open.One of the remarkable aspects of Federer's career is his longevity and consistency at the highest level of the sport. He remained a top contender well into his 30s, defying the usual career trajectory of many athletes.

Novak Djokovic

Novak Djokovic is a professional tennis player from Serbia and is considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Djokovic was born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade, Serbia.Djokovic has won numerous Grand Slam singles titles. He has held all four Grand Slam titles simultaneously, achieving a Career Grand Slam, and he has won each major multiple times.Djokovic has particularly excelled at the Australian Open, where he has won multiple titles. His success on hard courts, including those at the Australian Open, is a significant part of his tennis legacy.
